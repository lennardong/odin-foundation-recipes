Microwave Noodles

Who says you can't have quality, cost and speed all at once? This is comfort food at its best. If you're feeling extra special, use SsangYong brand noodles

Ingredients

  • packet of instant noodles
  • sesame oil
  • raw egg

Recipe

  1. Put noodle in bowl you will eat
  2. Crack egg, add water and a few dashes of sesame oil
  3. Cover top and microwave for 1-2 minutes
  4. Be a good role model. Eat out of sight from children
